Transforming the Modern Classroom

We built this system to bridge the gap between education and technology. By leveraging Artificial Intelligence, we eliminate the friction of traditional classroom management.

    Reclaims valuable lecture time previously lost to manual roll calls.

Powerful Capabilities

Designed to provide a seamless experience for both professors and students.

Face Recognition

High-accuracy facial mapping to identify multiple students simultaneously in real-time.

QR Code Attendance

Dynamic, time-sensitive QR codes for rapid scanning directly from student mobile devices.

Real-time Dashboard

Live monitoring interfaces for professors to track attendance status instantly during lectures.

Attendance Analytics

Comprehensive data visualization, trend tracking, and automated Excel report generation.

Secure Data Management

Encrypted data storage ensuring student privacy and adherence to institutional protocols.
